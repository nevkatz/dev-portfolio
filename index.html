About Me

I am a web and app developer based in the Boston area with 15 years of experience building websites and mobile applications. At Education Development Center (EDC), I’ve worked extensively with Drupal and am starting to build in WordPress. I am skilled in JavaScript, CSS, and PHP, as well as mobile frameworks such as Cordova and Capacitor. This portfolio brings together selected EDC projects and independent work that reflect both my technical skills and design sensibilities..
